Short feature list of Karbari app

Karbari app's features give you the ultimate flexibility

Courier Integration

  • Integrates seamlessly with various courier services.
  • Automatically updates courier notes, prices, and final charges.

Real-Time Order Tracking

  • Provides live updates on order status for both merchants and customers.
  • Enhances customer trust with transparent delivery processes.

Automated Inventory Management

  • Monitors and updates inventory levels automatically.
  • Sends alerts for low stock to prevent product shortages.

WordPress Plugin Integration

  • Features a WordPress plugin to sync orders from WooCommerce sites directly into the app.
  • Automates the update of order data whenever an order is placed on the website.

Multi-User Environment

  • Allows multiple users with different access permissions to enhance collaboration.
  • Supports role-based access to system features and data.

Out of the box Customization Capabilities

  • Offers extensive customization to suit specific business workflows and needs.
  • Adaptable to different e-commerce and f-commerce business models.

Advanced Fraud Detection

  • Utilizes advanced algorithms to identify and prevent fraudulent orders.
  • Protects against financial losses and preserves business integrity.

Cloud-Based Application

Embrace simplicity with Karbari's cloud-based solution. No installation required—access your business tools from anywhere, anytime. Our platform eliminates the need for complex setups, letting you focus on what matters most: growing your business.

Super Fast Browsing Experience

Speed is essential, and Karbari delivers it in spades. Our application is hosted at edge locations nearest to you, ensuring lightning-fast access and responsiveness. Thanks to dedicated resource allocations, each user enjoys an isolated and optimized environment, making your experience smooth and efficient.

Daily Data Backup

Secure, Reliable, and Hassle-Free

Backups Stored Off-Site

Ensuring your data's safety goes beyond regular backups; it's about where these backups are stored. At Karbari, we store all backups off-site, away from our data centers. This strategy protects your valuable data against local disasters, ensuring that even in the worst scenarios, your business can recover swiftly and completely.

Daily and Fully Automatic

Forget manual backups and the risks of human error. Our system performs automatic backups every day, at no extra cost to you. This process is fully automated, providing you with peace of mind and the assurance that your latest business data is always safe and retrievable.

Hourly Backup Options

For businesses that require more frequent data protection, Karbari offers hourly backups. This service ensures that even the most recent transactions are safeguarded, minimizing data loss and providing continuous protection for your critical operations.

Seamless Courier Integration

Integrated Directly into Karbari

Both Pathao and Steadfast are fully integrated into the Karbari app, providing a seamless and streamlined logistics experience. Manage shipments, track deliveries in real-time, and access detailed reports all from one platform. Our courier integrations are designed to simplify your operations, reduce overhead, and improve overall customer satisfaction.
